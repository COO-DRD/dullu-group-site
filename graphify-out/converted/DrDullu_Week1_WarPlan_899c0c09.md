DR. DULLU
WEEK 1 WAR PLAN
3 June – 8 June 2026  |  Kilifi, Kenya

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

THE SITUATION
Location: Kilifi. Cash: 0 KES. Claude subscription: running out. Internet: stable.
This is not a comfortable planning exercise. This is a survival week. Every action this week has one filter: does it move toward cash in hand by Saturday?
The brand is built. The tools exist. DDi is live. 4unter is running. The LinkedIn post is up. The only variable now is execution.

THE ONLY GOAL THIS WEEK

What counts as a win:
- $231 onboarding + $96/mo Starter retainer (DDi subscription)  =  immediate cash
- $900 custom website build, 50% upfront  =  $450 in hand
- Any paid freelance dev work via Upwork or direct referral
What does not count as a win this week: followers, profile views, people saying "interesting", anyone asking to "catch up soon."


FIRST THING   Post the LinkedIn post
It is already written. Open LinkedIn, paste it, post it. Do not edit it. Do not wait for the perfect moment. Post it now.
MORNING   Map your warm network
Open your contacts. Write down every person who: (a) runs a business, (b) works at a business that needs a website or systems, or (c) knows someone who does. Target: 20 names minimum. Do not filter too hard.
MIDDAY   Send personal WhatsApp to all 20
One by one. Not broadcast. Personal. Use the script below.
WhatsApp script — network activation
TO SOMEONE WHO RUNS A BUSINESS
"Hey [name], quick one. I'm running DDi now, we build websites and automation systems for businesses. Do you need your site updated or are you losing leads somewhere? Looking for 2 clients this week specifically."
TO SOMEONE WHO MIGHT KNOW A BUSINESS OWNER
"Hey [name], do you know anyone who runs a business and has been complaining their website is outdated or they're struggling to follow up with customers? I'm looking for 2 clients this week and would appreciate an intro."
AFTERNOON   Set up Upwork profile
Title: Web Development, Automation & AI Systems Specialist. Rate: $25/hr to start. Add digital.dullugroup.co.ke as portfolio link. Write a short bio using the Dr. Dullu origin story. This takes one hour max. Do not overthink it.
EVENING   Reply to every response from the WhatsApp blast
Anyone who engages gets a response within 30 minutes. Speed signals seriousness. If someone shows interest, book a call or offer to show them the DDi site.
End of day check
- LinkedIn post is live
- 20+ WhatsApp messages sent
- Upwork profile live
- At least 3 conversations started


MORNING   Run 4unter on Kilifi + Mombasa
Target: hotels, tour operators, real estate agencies, clinics, logistics companies. Pull 100 leads. You are not sending today — you are building the list you will use Thursday and Friday if the warm network has not converted yet.
MIDDAY   Follow up every Tuesday conversation
Anyone who replied but did not commit gets a follow-up. Not a reminder — a value add. Send them one specific thing: a screenshot of the DDi site, a line about what you would fix for their specific business, or a voice note. Voice notes get replied to.
AFTERNOON   First close attempt
If anyone from Tuesday is warm, push for a decision today. The script below.
Closing script — Starter retainer
WHEN THEY SHOW INTEREST
"I have a Starter package that covers [specific thing relevant to their business]. Setup is $231 and then $96 a month. I can have the first version live within 7 days. Want to go ahead?"
WHEN THEY SAY "SEND ME MORE INFO"
"I'll send you the site link. The one thing I'd ask is — what's the biggest thing your business is losing time or customers on right now? That way I can show you exactly what we'd fix."
EVENING   Bid on 3 Upwork jobs
Search: automation, web development, AI tools, Python scripting. Bid on jobs under $500. Write a specific proposal — reference the exact job, mention one relevant thing you've built, name a delivery timeline. Generic proposals get ignored.


MORNING   Voice note follow-up to anyone who has not replied
Anyone from Tuesday or Wednesday who read your message but did not reply gets a 30-second voice note. "Hey [name], just wanted to check if you had a second to look at that. Happy to jump on a quick call if easier." Voice notes get opened. Text gets ignored.
MIDDAY   Start firing 4unter leads via personal WhatsApp
Take 30 leads from the Kilifi/Mombasa list. Send personal messages from your number. Not bulk. One by one. Reference something specific about their business. "Hey, I checked your site and noticed you don't have a booking system — is that something you've been looking to fix?"
AFTERNOON   If you have one yes: take the deposit immediately
Do not wait for a signed contract. Do not wait for a bank transfer to clear. M-Pesa or PayPal or whatever works. Take 50% upfront and start. Speed builds trust. Delays kill deals.
EVENING   If no close yet: post a second LinkedIn post
Short one. "Day 3 of the week. Here is what I am building and who I am looking for." Specific. Direct. Ends with a CTA to DM you.
If someone asks what you can do for them — the fast answer
FOR A BUSINESS WITH NO WEBSITE OR A BAD ONE
"I build you a fast, modern website on Next.js. Live in 7 days. Starts at $900 for full ownership or $231 + $96/mo on our retainer."
FOR A BUSINESS LOSING LEADS OR RUNNING MANUALLY
"I map where you are losing customers and build the automation that fixes it. Booking systems, lead pipelines, follow-up sequences. Starts at $231 onboarding."


MORNING   Every open conversation gets a final push
If someone has been "thinking about it" for 3 days, give them a reason to decide today. "I have one spot open this week. If you want to move I can start today. If not, no problem — I'll circle back next month."  Scarcity is honest here. You actually only have time for one or two clients right now.
MIDDAY   Check Upwork for replies to Wednesday bids
Reply within the hour to any Upwork message. Upwork clients test responsiveness before they hire. Fast reply = first impression of how you work.
AFTERNOON   If client closed: start delivering
Do not wait until Monday. Start the discovery, the wireframe, the setup. Showing immediate momentum after payment closes the psychological loop and makes referrals more likely.
AFTERNOON   If no close yet: review what is not working
Is it the offer? The price? The audience? The channel? Adjust one variable and try again. Do not change everything at once.
EVENING   Post progress on LinkedIn
"End of week 1. Here is what happened." Honest. Specific. Even if no client closed, the story of the attempt is content. This is the building-in-public strategy in action.


MORNING   Follow up any remaining conversations
Last push. Anyone still on the fence gets a simple message: "Are we moving forward or should I focus elsewhere?" Respect their time and yours.
MIDDAY   If client closed: ask for one referral
"Do you know one other business owner who could use this?" You ask after they are happy with the decision, not after delivery. Ask now while the yes energy is still fresh.
AFTERNOON   Count the week
Leads contacted. Conversations started. Proposals sent. Calls had. Closes. Revenue. Write it down. This is your baseline. Week 2 beats it.
EVENING   Plan week 2
If week 1 closed: week 2 is about delivery + pipeline. If week 1 did not close: week 2 doubles the outreach volume and adds one new channel.
